Forbidden Territories

Music censorship and discrimination

Our main goals with that project are:

  • Letting the artists know which words in what context and which lyrics are / were censored in every country.
  • Letting the artists know which in what context and which lyrics could be censored through the censorship predictive model.
  • Visualize censorship through data in every country.
  • Visualize which words are trending topics around the globe.
