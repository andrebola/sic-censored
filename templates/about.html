project and team

Forbidden Territories is a project developed by Rita Geleta, Andrés Ferraro, Alberto González Pulido, Sylvain Le Groux and Gema FB Martin during the sónar+D innovation challenge (SIC) in November 2020

Our main goals with that project are:

_Letting the artists know which words in what context and which lyrics are/were censored in every country.

_Letting the artists know which in what context and which lyrics could be censored through the censorship predictive model.

_Visualize censorship through data in every country.

_Visualize which words are trending topics around the globe.
